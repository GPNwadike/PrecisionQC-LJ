Corrective action escalation ladder
1
Rerun the control
Rule out a simple random error or handling mistake. Do not report patient results until the control passes.
2
Use fresh materials
Open a new vial of control material and/or fresh reagent to rule out degradation or contamination.
3
Recalibrate the instrument
Run a full calibration sequence if fresh materials still fail.
4
Perform maintenance
Execute deep cleaning, lamp check, or hardware maintenance as indicated by the error type.
5
Document everything
Log the rule violated, root cause identified, and all corrective actions taken in the QC ledger before resuming patient testing.
